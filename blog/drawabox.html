Drawabox Lessons

Drawabox is a free online drawing course, via a website, that focuses on providing a structured approach to learning how to draw. It has 7 lessons, as of this writing, which are very dense and cover a range of topics from basic line sketching to drawing objects and figures using construction lines. I started taking the course a few months ago and just recently completed lesson 1. It's hard, but enjoyable and my skills have definitely gotten better. Drawabox requires each student to read the course material and do the corresponding homework, which can then be critiqued by members of the community on either the website or Drawabox's discord channel.

Over the next few months, as I complete the remaining lessons, I'll post my homework along with lesson catalogs and other stuff on this page. Starting with lesson 1 below.
